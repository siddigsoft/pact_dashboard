# PACT Workflow Platform
## Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with PACT in 5 minutes!

---

## Step 1: Access the Platform

Open your web browser and navigate to the PACT platform URL. You'll see the Command Center landing page.

## Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Click **"Get Started"**
2. Click **"Register"** 
3. Enter your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
4. Select your role (ask your administrator which role applies to you)
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. Check your email and click the verification link

## Step 3: Log In

1. Return to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

1. Click your avatar in the top right corner
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Upload a profile photo
4. Add your contact details
5. Save changes

## Step 5: Explore Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows information based on your role:

| Your Role | What You'll See |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full system overview, all pending approvals |
| **Coordinator** | Your assigned sites, pending visits |
| **Data Collector** | Your site visit assignments |
| **FOM** | Field team status, MMP progress |
| **Finance** | Budget status, pending payments |

---

## For Data Collectors: Your First Site Visit

1. View your **Assigned Visits** on the dashboard
2. Click on a pending visit
3. Click **"Start Visit"**
4. Allow location access when prompted
5. Capture required data and photos
6. Click **"Complete Visit"**
7. Submit cost documentation if required

---

## For Coordinators: Viewing Your Sites

1. Go to **Site Visits** in the sidebar
2. View all sites assigned to you
3. Filter by status: Pending, In Progress, Completed
4. Click any visit to see details
5. Monitor your team's progress

---

## For Admins: Uploading an MMP

1. Go to **MMP** > **Upload**
2. Select your project
3. Choose the monitoring period
4. Upload your CSV file
5. Review validation results
6. Click **"Upload"** to confirm
7. Forward to FOM for dispatching

## Getting Help

- Click the **?** icon anywhere for contextual help
- Contact your administrator for role changes
- Review the full User Manual for detailed instructions

---

## Top Tips

1. **Enable notifications** to stay updated on assignments
2. **Allow location access** for accurate GPS capture
3. **Sync regularly** when working offline on mobile
4. **Upload receipts promptly** for faster cost approvals
5. **Use filters** to find specific items quickly
